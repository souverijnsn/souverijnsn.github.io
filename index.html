3D animations of heat stress in Johannesburg and Ekurhuleni

3D animations of heat stress are made available for a few neighborhoods in Johannesburg and Ekurhuleni. These were generated in the framework of the Technical project: Facilitation of Community-Driven Urban Heat Monitoring Campaign. Heat stress for the 6th of January 2016 at 15h local time is visualised, corresponding to the peak of a record-breaking heatwave over the region.

Depending on your internet connection, it might take some time to load the animation. Please be patient.
